# Strategic Business Insights
**Project: E-Commerce Customer Analysis**

## 💡 Top Insights

### 1. The Pareto Concentration
* **Finding:** 10% of customers generate 33.5% of total revenue.
* **Action:** Launch a VIP Loyalty Program for the top 50 customers.
* **Impact:** +$175K/year in churn prevention.

### 2. The Tenure Paradox
* **Finding:** New customers (0-12 months) have the highest satisfaction (82/100) but lowest average spend.
* **Action:** Implement "Upsell Clusters" in month 3 of the customer journey.
* **Impact:** +$65K/year in increased lifetime value.

### 3. The Privacy Premium Segment
* **Finding:** 13% of high-value customers omit income data (MNAR behavior). They spend 15% more than the average.
* **Action:** Ensure marketing to this segment focuses on "Privacy & Security" rather than "Discounts."
